Ebola Words

Classic

Ebola Words Is A Game Where You Have Text That You Have To Cure Of Ebola

When You Play Classic Mode, You Will Be Given The First Sentence Of A Random Wikipedia Article

Very Quickly, Words Will Get Ebola (and turn red)

In Order To "Cure" These Words, You Must Click Them

After You Cure 10 Words, The Text Will Expand Into A Second Paragraph

The Article Will Continue To Get Bigger Every 10 Words

After Curing 30 Words, You Will Unlock A Wipeout

A Wipeout Will Cure All Words Of Ebola, And Can Be Used If You're Ever In Trouble

If 10 Words Get Ebola At The Same Time, You Lose

Triple Ebola

Triple Ebola Is Similar To Ebola Words, Except Words Can Get Double And Triple Ebola

When A Word Gets Double Ebola, You Will Have To Click It Twice

When A Word Get Triple Ebola, You Will Have To Click It Three Times

Time Attack

In Time Attack, You'll Have A Timer Counting Down

When It Reaches Zero, You Lose

You'll Be Given A Small Amount Of Text With 10 Words With Ebola

When You Click (cure) All Ten, The Timer Will Increase A Bit, But There Will Be More Text And More Words With Ebola
