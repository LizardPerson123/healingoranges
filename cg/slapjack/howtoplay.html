Slapjack

Slapjack Is A Card Game

In Slapjack, Everyone Playing Places A Card Onto A Deck, One After Another

If The Card Placed Is A Jack, Whoever Slaps The Jack First Wins All The Cards In The Deck

Once Someone Has All The Cards, They Win

To Slap The Jack, Click The Card

If You Slap A Card That Is Not A Jack, You Lose 2 Cards (That Is Only In This Version)
