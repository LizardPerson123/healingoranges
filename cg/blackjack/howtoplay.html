Blackjack

Blackjack Is An Old Card Game

In Blackjack, You Are Competing With A Dealer

Both You And The Dealer Will Be Given 2 Cards

You Will Be Able To See Both Your Cards, But Only The Dealer's First Card

Each Card Corresponds To It's Number, With The Following Exceptions

  • Aces Can Be Either 1 OR 11 (Which Ever One Gets You The Highest Number)
  • Kings, Queens, And Jacks All Equal 10

To Win, You Need To Have A Higher Number Than The Dealer, Without Going Over 21

If You Go Over 21, You Bust (Lose Instantly)

In Order To Increase Your Number, You Need To "Hit", Which Will Add A Card

Once You're Satisfied With Your Number, You Can Stay (Which Ends Your Turn)

The Dealer Will Then Reveal Their Second Card

If The Dealer's Number Is Lower Than 16, Then They Will Draw Another Card

If You're Number Is Higher Than The Dealer, Then You Win

If It's Lower, You Lose

Tokens

When You First Play Blackjack, You Start With 5 Tokens

Before You Begin A Round, You Will Gamble Some Of Those Tokens

If You Win, You Win That Amount Of Tokens

But If You Lose, You Lose Those Tokens
