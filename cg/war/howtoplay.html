War

War Is A Two Player Card Game

In War, You Have Two Players Who Each Get Half The Deck

The Two Players Will Both Place A Card At The Same Time

Whoever Has The Higher Number Card Will Get Both The Cards

If Both Cards Are The Same Value, You Enter A War

Both Players Will Place 3 Additional Cards

Then, They Will Both Place Another Card, Whoever Has The Highest Value Gets All 4 Cards

Once One Player Has All The Cards, They Win
